HOME/ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Physics Department of MANIT Bhopal is one of the founding Department of the Institute since 1960 when the institute was Maulana Azad College of Technology (MACT). The department runs an M.Tech. course in Nanotechnology since 2006 with an intake of 18 students. A center of Nanoscience and Engineering is formed within the department in 2014 The Department and the centre is engaged in research leading to Ph.D. and teaching in the frontier areas of experimental sciences. These include Nanoscience & Nanotechnology, condensed matter physics and materials science

Excellence in research and teaching has been the hallmark of this Department. Apart from our own research in the leading edge since its inception, the Department has, over the years, produced some of the brightest minds in Physics, active both in the country and all over the world, with several fundamental contributions in the field.

Programme Educational Objectives(PEOs)

Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs) of the programme M.Tech. in Nanotechnology addresses wide aspects of Nanotechnology in line with the Departmentâ€™s vision and mission. These are:

PEO 1: Building fundamental concepts about low dimensional materials and their properties

PEO 2: To promote research in the budding and burgeoning areas of nanotechnology through nanomaterials with innovative properties and novel synthesis methods.

PEO 3: To instil analytical skills in the area of continuously evolving nanomaterials for its applications in green technology, solar energy, environment, drug delivery etc.

PEO 4: To inculcate leadership with team spirit, managerial skills, moral and ethical values.

Program Outcomes

PO1: Graduating student will able to demonstrate a degree of mastery over Nanotechnology and its application with the knowledge of basic science, engineering higher than a bachelor of science/engineering program

PO2: Graduating student will have an ability to apply analytical skills to understand and manipulate the properties of continuously evolving nanomaterials

PO3: Graduating students will be able to independently carry out interdisciplinary research /investigation and development work to solve practical problems through nanotechnology/nano materials.

PO4: Graduating student will have an ability to write and present a substantial technical report/document in the form of abstract/research paper

PO5: Graduating students demonstrate ethical considerations and concern for society and environment to promote sustainable development.

VISION

Vision

Stimulate interdisciplinary research, including vertical integration between basic sciences and engineering to promote links with society and industry.

MISSION

Mission

Develop state of art educational and research environment, impart knowledge in all areas of life through transfer of fundamental technology. Develop skills amongst faculty and students to meet the demands of the industry to face the global challenges.
